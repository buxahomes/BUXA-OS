# AGENTS.md

# BUXA Business Operating System Repository

This repository contains the official Business Operating System (BOS) for BUXA.

Every AI agent working inside this repository should treat the contents as the authoritative source of business knowledge.

---

# Mission

Your responsibility is to strengthen the Business Operating System.

Do not create isolated solutions.

Every contribution should improve consistency, reusability and long-term maintainability.

---

# Working Style

When solving problems:

1. Understand before changing.
2. Reuse before creating.
3. Simplify before expanding.
4. Preserve before replacing.
5. Explain before implementing.

Prefer incremental improvements over large rewrites.

---

# Repository Hierarchy

Before making changes, understand the hierarchy of authority.

```
People
        │
        ▼
Business Knowledge
        │
        ▼
Business Governance
        │
        ▼
Artificial Intelligence
        │
        ▼
Automation
```

Authority always flows from top to bottom.

AI must never override higher levels.

# Repository Goals

This repository aims to become:

- The official Business Operating System of BUXA.
- The Single Source of Truth for business knowledge.
- The foundation for AI Workforce.
- The foundation for Automation.
- The foundation for future software systems.

Every contribution should move the repository closer to these goals.

---

# Business Knowledge

Business Knowledge is the Single Source of Truth.

Never invent:

- products
- specifications
- terminology
- workflows
- governance
- policies

When uncertain, ask for clarification.

Do not guess.

---

# Design Principles

Every change should follow the Design Principles.

Always prefer:

- reuse over duplication
- consistency over convenience
- evolution over replacement

Do not introduce competing concepts.

---

# Terminology

Always use the official definitions in the Glossary.

Never redefine existing concepts.

If a new concept is required:

- explain why
- propose the definition
- wait for approval

---

# Documentation Rules

Every document should:

- have one clear purpose
- belong to one module
- avoid duplicated definitions
- reference existing knowledge where appropriate
- remain reusable

Do not create unnecessary documentation.

---

# Writing Standards

Write using:

- clear English
- concise language
- reusable structure
- consistent terminology

Avoid:

- marketing language
- unnecessary repetition
- ambiguous wording

Business documentation should prioritise clarity.

---

# Architecture Rules

Protect the existing architecture.

Do not:

- move modules without reason
- rename concepts casually
- duplicate knowledge
- break cross-module consistency

Architecture evolves intentionally.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence should:

- retrieve knowledge
- transform knowledge
- improve consistency
- identify gaps
- suggest improvements

Artificial Intelligence should never redefine official Business Knowledge.

---

# Human Approval

Human approval is required before:

- changing Business Knowledge
- changing Design Principles
- changing Governance
- changing Product Passports
- changing Brand Positioning
- introducing new architecture
- deleting official documentation

Prepare recommendations.

People approve decisions.

---

# Engineering Principles

Prefer:

- simple solutions
- modular structure
- reusable components
- maintainable documentation

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

# Commit Philosophy

Every contribution should improve at least one of the following:

- clarity
- consistency
- maintainability
- reusability
- governance
- knowledge quality

Do not make changes without measurable improvement.

---

# Definition of Done

A task is complete only when:

- Business Knowledge remains correct.
- Terminology remains consistent.
- Cross-module consistency is preserved.
- Documentation remains reusable.
- Architecture becomes stronger.
- The Business Operating System improves.

Completion means improving the system, not simply finishing the task.

---

# Repository Philosophy

Knowledge is the foundation.

Assets create capability.

Artificial Intelligence scales execution.

Automation coordinates workflows.

People remain responsible.

Every contribution should strengthen the Business Operating System.
