# Purpose

This document defines how business knowledge is transformed into multiple content assets within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Content Repurposing is not the duplication of existing content.

It is the systematic transformation of official business knowledge into different formats for different audiences, channels and business objectives.

The purpose of Content Repurposing is to maximise the long-term value of business knowledge while maintaining consistency across all communication.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to create content once from official business knowledge and continuously transform it into valuable communication across the Business Operating System.

Knowledge should become increasingly valuable through reuse.

Content should become increasingly efficient through transformation.

---

# Repurposing Philosophy

Business knowledge remains permanent.

Core content communicates knowledge.

Repurposing adapts communication.

Different formats support different audiences.

Customer understanding creates confidence.

Business learning improves future transformation.

Repurposing exists to multiply the value of business knowledge rather than increase content production.

# Repurposing Principles

Every repurposed content asset should:

- Begin with official business knowledge.
- Preserve terminology consistency.
- Support one business objective.
- Match one audience.
- Fit one communication channel.
- Strengthen customer confidence.

Repurposing should adapt communication without changing business knowledge.

---

# Core Content

Every knowledge topic should first become one authoritative core content asset.

Examples include:

- Buying Guide
- Product Guide
- PDMS Guide
- Installation Guide
- Style Guide
- FAQ
- Case Study

Core content becomes the source for future transformation.

Knowledge should become increasingly accessible without becoming inconsistent.

The objective is not more content.

# Repurposing Validation

Before repurposing any content, confirm:

- Does official business knowledge exist?
- Has terminology remained consistent?
- Does the new format match the audience?
- Does the selected channel support the objective?
- Will customer understanding improve?
- Can business learning be captured?

Repurposing should always increase business value.
