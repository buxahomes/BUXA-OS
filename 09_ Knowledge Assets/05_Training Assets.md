# Purpose

This document defines how Training Assets are created, governed and reused within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Training Assets are not simply presentations, manuals or onboarding documents.

They are reusable business assets designed to develop consistent business capabilities through official business knowledge.

The purpose of Training Assets is to ensure that every employee, partner and AI system develops the same business understanding, operational standards and execution capability.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build a capability-driven organisation.

Training should not depend on individual experience.

Business knowledge should be transformed into reusable training assets that consistently develop organisational capability.

Every Training Asset should improve execution quality.

---

# Training Asset Philosophy

Business knowledge defines standards.

Training Assets develop capability.

Capability improves execution.

Execution generates learning.

Learning evolves business knowledge.

Training exists to build capability rather than simply transfer information.

# Training Asset Principles

Every Training Asset should:

- Originate from official business knowledge.
- Develop one or more business capabilities.
- Preserve terminology consistency.
- Follow Business Operating System standards.
- Be reusable across teams.
- Support continuous organisational learning.

Training Assets should improve capability rather than simply increase knowledge.

# Training Asset Validation

Before creating or updating any Training Asset, confirm:

- Does it originate from official business knowledge?
- Does it develop a defined business capability?
- Is terminology consistent?
- Does it follow Business Operating System standards?
- Can it be reused?
- Will it improve execution quality?

Every Training Asset should strengthen organisational capability.
