# Purpose

This document defines how Product Assets are created, governed and reused within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Product Assets are not simply product files or marketing materials.

They are reusable business assets derived from official product knowledge and designed to support product understanding, confident product selection and successful project delivery.

The purpose of Product Assets is to ensure that every customer, designer, builder and AI system works from the same authoritative product knowledge.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build a product knowledge system where every product asset originates from one official source of truth.

Product knowledge should be transformed into reusable assets that support every stage of the customer journey and every business capability.

Each Product Asset should strengthen confidence before purchase and consistency after delivery.

---

# Product Asset Philosophy

Official product knowledge defines the truth.

Product standards define consistency.

Product Assets communicate product knowledge.

Understanding builds confidence.

Confidence supports correct product selection.

Successful delivery reinforces trust.

Product Assets exist to preserve and multiply product knowledge rather than simply describe products.

      │               │                │

      └───────────────┼────────────────┘
                      ▼
             AI Product Knowledge
                      │
                      ▼

Every asset should originate from the Product Passport.

The Product Passport is the authoritative source.

All downstream assets inherit their knowledge from it.

# Product Asset Principles

Every Product Asset should:

- Originate from official product knowledge.
- Maintain specification accuracy.
- Preserve approved terminology.
- Be reusable across multiple business capabilities.
- Support customer confidence.
- Be version controlled and traceable.

Product Assets should strengthen business capability rather than duplicate knowledge.

# Product Asset Validation

Before creating or updating any Product Asset, confirm:

- Does it originate from official Product Knowledge?
- Are specifications accurate?
- Is terminology consistent?
- Does it improve customer understanding?
- Does it strengthen customer confidence?
- Can it be reused across multiple business capabilities?

Every Product Asset should increase business value through reuse.
