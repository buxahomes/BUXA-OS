# Purpose

This document defines how Knowledge Assets are governed throughout the BUXA Business Operating System.

Asset Governance is not simply the approval of files or documents.

It is the governance of reusable business assets to ensure consistency, ownership, integrity, traceability and long-term organisational value.

The purpose of Asset Governance is to protect official business knowledge while enabling efficient reuse across every business capability.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build a governed knowledge ecosystem where every business asset remains accurate, reusable and trusted.

Governance should preserve organisational capability without slowing business innovation.

Every governed asset should strengthen the Business Operating System.

---

Business knowledge defines truth.

# Governance Principles

Every Knowledge Asset should:

- Originate from official business knowledge.
- Have one accountable owner.
- Maintain version history.
- Remain traceable.
- Be searchable.
- Preserve terminology consistency.
- Support organisational capability.
- Be reusable across business functions.

Governance should protect knowledge rather than restrict collaboration.

---

# Governance Responsibilities

Every Knowledge Asset should have clearly defined ownership.

| Responsibility | Owner |
|----------------|-------|
| Business Knowledge | Business Owner |
| Asset Creation | Capability Owner |
| Asset Review | Domain Expert |
| Asset Governance | Knowledge Governance |
| Version Control | Asset Owner |
| AI Consumption | AI Workforce |
| Final Accountability | People |

Ownership should always be explicit.

Governance should never become anonymous.

# Governance Validation

Before approving any Knowledge Asset, confirm:

- Does it originate from official business knowledge?
- Is ownership clearly assigned?
- Is terminology consistent?
- Has Business QA been completed?
- Is version information current?
- Is the asset reusable?
- Does it strengthen organisational capability?

Governance should protect long-term business quality.
