# Purpose

This document defines how AI Assets are created, governed and reused within the BUXA Business Operating System.

AI Assets are not simply prompts, workflows or AI tools.

They are governed business assets that enable Artificial Intelligence to perform approved business capabilities consistently, accurately and responsibly.

The purpose of AI Assets is to ensure that AI execution remains aligned with official business knowledge, governance and organisational standards.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build an AI-native enterprise where Artificial Intelligence operates through reusable, governed business assets.

AI should not depend on isolated prompts or individual expertise.

Instead, AI Assets should enable consistent execution across every AI platform, workflow and business capability.

---

# AI Asset Philosophy

Business knowledge defines truth.

AI Assets package business capability.

Artificial Intelligence performs execution.

Business outcomes generate learning.

Learning improves AI Assets.

AI Assets exist to preserve and scale organisational capability rather than individual prompts.

# AI Asset Principles

Every AI Asset should:

- Originate from official business knowledge.
- Support one or more approved business capabilities.
- Follow AI Governance.
- Preserve terminology consistency.
- Be reusable across AI platforms.
- Be version controlled.
- Be traceable to its knowledge source.

AI Assets should strengthen organisational capability rather than individual AI performance.

---

- Cursor

- Future AI Platforms
- Internal AI Applications

AI Assets should remain platform-independent.

Business capability should outlast individual AI tools.

# AI Asset Validation

Before creating or updating any AI Asset, confirm:

- Does it originate from official business knowledge?
- Does it support a defined business capability?
- Is terminology consistent?
- Does it follow AI Governance?
- Can it be reused across platforms?
- Does it improve execution quality?

Every AI Asset should strengthen the Business Operating System.
