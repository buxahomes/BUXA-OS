# Purpose

This document defines how digital business assets are organised, governed, discovered and reused within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Digital Asset Management (DAM) is not simply the storage of files.

It is the management of reusable digital business assets derived from official business knowledge.

The purpose of Digital Asset Management is to maximise asset discoverability, consistency, reuse and long-term business value.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build a knowledge-centric Digital Asset Management system.

Every digital asset should originate from official business knowledge.

Every asset should be searchable, reusable and continuously improved through business learning.

Digital assets should strengthen organisational capability rather than accumulate as isolated files.

---

# Digital Asset Philosophy

Business knowledge creates assets.

Assets create business capability.

Digital Asset Management enables discovery.

Discovery enables reuse.

Reuse creates business value.

Business learning continuously improves digital assets.

Digital Asset Management exists to maximise organisational capability rather than file storage.

# Digital Asset Principles

Every digital asset should:

- Originate from official business knowledge.
- Have one authoritative owner.
- Be version controlled.
- Be searchable.
- Be reusable.
- Be traceable.
- Follow governance standards.

Digital assets should support business capability rather than isolated projects.

---

# Asset Metadata Standards

Every digital asset should include structured metadata.

Required metadata may include:

- Asset ID
- Asset Name
- Asset Category
- Business Module
- Owner
- Version
- Status
- Language
- Keywords
- Related Knowledge Source
- Last Updated

Metadata enables efficient discovery and governance.

# Digital Asset Validation

Before publishing any digital asset, confirm:

- Does it originate from official business knowledge?
- Is metadata complete?
- Is ownership defined?
- Is version information current?
- Can the asset be reused?
- Is it searchable?
- Does it support business capability?

Every digital asset should increase organisational value.
