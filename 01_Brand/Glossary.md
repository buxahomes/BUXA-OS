# Purpose

This document defines the official terminology used throughout the BUXA ecosystem.

Its purpose is to ensure that every employee, AI system, automation workflow, website, marketing campaign, and business document uses the same language consistently.

Every official term has one approved definition.

When a term is defined here, it should be used consistently across all BOS documents.

# Product Terms

## Engineered Timber Flooring

The official product category offered by BUXA.

Preferred term.

Avoid using "Engineered Flooring" when referring to official product descriptions.

---

## European Oak

The hardwood veneer used in BUXA engineered flooring products.

---

## Skirting Board

Interior wall finishing component designed to cover expansion gaps and protect walls.

---

## Trim & Finishing

Collective term for transition profiles and finishing accessories.

Includes:

- Stair Nosing
- T-Mould
- Reducer
- End Cap

---

## Curated Sample Box

Official name for BUXA's sample program.

Represents carefully selected flooring collections designed to help customers compare colours, textures and finishes.

---

# Marketing Terms

## Factory Direct

Products supplied directly from the manufacturer without traditional retail distribution.

Represents value through supply chain efficiency rather than lower quality.

---

## Designed for Australian Homes

Official positioning phrase highlighting that products and specifications are developed specifically for Australian residential projects.

---

## Premium

Represents quality, professionalism, reliability and long-term value.

Premium does not imply luxury for luxury's sake.

---

## Timeless

Design that remains relevant beyond short-term trends.

---

## Professional

Work performed through standards, systems and consistent execution.

---

## Architectural

Related to design quality, proportion, material selection and long-term aesthetics.

Avoid using the term purely as a marketing adjective.

# Terms to Avoid

The following expressions do not align with the BUXA brand.

Examples include:

- Cheap
- Lowest Price
- Best Deal
- Amazing Quality
- Perfect Flooring
- Luxury Flooring (unless specifically describing a project style)
- Unbelievable
- Revolutionary
- Guaranteed Best

These terms reduce credibility and do not reflect the BUXA communication philosophy.

---

# AI Usage Rules

When generating content, AI should always:

- Use official terminology from this document.
- Avoid creating alternative names for official products or brands.
- Prefer consistency over creativity when referring to brand assets.
- Follow Brand Communication Standards when choosing vocabulary.
