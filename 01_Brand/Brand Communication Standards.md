# Brand Communication Standards

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Owner: Founder

Review Cycle: Every 12 Months

---

# Purpose

Define how BUXA communicates across every platform.

Every employee, AI agent and external partner must follow these standards.

---

# Communication Philosophy

BUXA communicates like an experienced design partner.

Not like a salesperson.

We educate.

We guide.

We build confidence.

We never pressure customers.

---

# Brand Personality

Professional

Elegant

Confident

Calm

Knowledgeable

Helpful

Timeless

Human

---

# Tone of Voice

Professional

Clear

Simple

Educational

Confident

Warm

Honest

Never exaggerated.

Never arrogant.

Never loud.

---

# Writing Style

Short sentences.

Simple language.

Avoid unnecessary adjectives.

Focus on clarity.

Use active voice whenever possible.

---

Lowest Price

Unbelievable

Amazing

Perfect

Revolutionary

Guaranteed

---

# Headlines

Should be clear.

Should educate.

Should inspire.

Should never feel like advertising.

Examples:

How to Choose Timber Flooring

Why Samples Matter

Designed for Australian Homes

Helping Designers Deliver Better Homes

---

# Calls to Action

Preferred：

Explore the Collection

Learn More

Order a Sample Box

Discover More

View the Guide

Avoid：

Buy Now

Limited Time

Huge Discount

Don't Miss Out

Act Fast

# AI Rules

Avoid hype.

Avoid clickbait.

Avoid unnecessary marketing language.
