# Purpose

This module defines how business execution is coordinated within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Automation is not the replacement of human work.

It is the orchestration of business capabilities through structured workflows triggered by business events.

The purpose of this module is to ensure that business activities are executed consistently, efficiently and continuously while remaining aligned with official business knowledge, governance and human responsibility.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build an event-driven business where routine work is coordinated automatically while people remain focused on strategy, creativity and decision-making.

Automation should connect business knowledge, AI Workforce and business systems into one continuously operating Business Operating System.

Business execution should become repeatable.

Business quality should remain consistent.

Business learning should continuously improve every workflow.

---

# Automation Philosophy

Business events initiate work.

Workflows coordinate business capabilities.

Artificial Intelligence performs specialised tasks.

People provide judgement where required.

Automation executes repeatable business processes.

Business learning improves future workflows.

Automation exists to coordinate execution rather than replace business ownership.

# Business Events

Automation should always begin with a business event.

Business events represent meaningful changes that require business execution.

Examples include:

- New customer enquiry
- Product updated
- Website published
- Marketing campaign launched
- Inspection completed
- Project stage changed
- Customer feedback received
- Sales opportunity created

Business events determine when workflows should begin.

Time-based scheduling is only one type of business event.

The Business Operating System should become increasingly event-driven over time.
