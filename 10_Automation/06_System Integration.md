# Purpose

This document defines how business systems integrate within the BUXA Business Operating System.

System Integration is not the connection of software applications.

It is the connection of business capabilities with the systems that support business execution.

The purpose of System Integration is to ensure that every connected platform contributes to one consistent Business Operating System.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build one integrated business ecosystem.

Business systems should exchange information through shared business knowledge rather than isolated software integrations.

Technology may evolve.

Business capabilities remain consistent.

---

# Integration Philosophy

Business capabilities define work.

Business systems provide operational data.

Integration connects business capabilities with operational systems.

Artificial Intelligence applies business knowledge.

Automation coordinates execution.

Integration should strengthen business consistency rather than increase technical complexity.

---

# Integration Architecture

Every integration follows one consistent architecture.

```text
Business Capability
        │
        ▼
Integration Layer
        │
        ▼
Business Systems
        │
        ▼
Business Data
        │
        ▼
Business Execution
```

Business capabilities remain independent of software platforms.

---

# Integration Principles

Every integration should:

- Support one business capability.
- Exchange official business data.
- Respect AI Governance.
- Preserve terminology consistency.
- Follow Workflow Standards.
- Contribute to Business Learning.

Integration should never become the source of business knowledge.

# Integration Priority

Business systems should be integrated according to business value rather than technical convenience.

Recommended integration order:

1. Business Knowledge
2. Customer Communication
3. Website
4. Marketing
5. Project Delivery
6. Analytics
7. Enterprise Systems

Every new integration should strengthen existing business capabilities before introducing additional complexity.

Integration should always follow Business Operating System priorities.

---

# Integration Validation

Before connecting any business system, Automation should confirm:

- Which business capability is supported?
- What business data will be exchanged?
- Does the integration follow governance?
- Is terminology consistent?
- Is Business QA required?
- Does the integration improve business execution?

Technology should always support business objectives.
