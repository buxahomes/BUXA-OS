# Purpose

This document defines the role, responsibilities and operating principles of Artificial Intelligence within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

Artificial Intelligence exists to support project delivery by improving efficiency, knowledge accessibility and documentation quality.

Artificial Intelligence never replaces professional judgement.

---

# AI Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence is an assistant.

Not an inspector.

Not a project manager.

Not a decision maker.

Within BUXA PDMS™, Artificial Intelligence exists to support professionals rather than replace them.

Technology empowers craftsmanship.

It never replaces it.

---

# Core Principle

AI Assists.

Professionals Decide.

The Flooring Project Engineer remains accountable for:

- Inspection decisions
- Quality acceptance
- Project delivery
- Customer communication

Artificial Intelligence never assumes professional responsibility.

---

# The Role of AI

Artificial Intelligence supports four core areas.

Knowledge

↓

Workflow

↓

Documentation

↓

Project Intelligence

Professional judgement remains outside the responsibility of Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Knowledge Assistant

Artificial Intelligence helps users quickly access project knowledge.

Examples include:

- Inspection Standards
- Control Points
- Installation Guides
- Material Information
- Historical Projects
- Best Practices
- Frequently Asked Questions

The objective is to reduce the time required to locate information.

---

# Workflow Assistant

Artificial Intelligence helps the Flooring Project Engineer navigate project workflows.

Examples include:

- Explain the next task
- Explain Quality Gates
- Explain project stages
- Explain inspection procedures
- Recommend workflow documents

Workflow decisions remain under the control of the Flooring Project Engineer.

---

# Documentation Assistant

Artificial Intelligence may automatically:

- Generate observations
- Summarise inspections
- Organise photographs
- Draft daily reports
- Draft inspection reports
- Generate project summaries
- Prepare Project Evidence Packages

The Flooring Project Engineer reviews all generated content before approval.

---

# Project Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence analyses project information to provide insights.

Examples include:

- Historical trends
- Similar project references
- Frequently occurring defects
- Outstanding risks
- Progress summaries
- Project statistics

Artificial Intelligence provides information rather than conclusions.

---

# AI Assistant

Within BUXA PDMS™, users interact with Artificial Intelligence through the BUXA Assistant.

Typical requests include:

- Explain this inspection standard.
- Find similar projects.
- Show the installation guide.
- Explain this defect.
- Summarise today's inspections.
- Generate today's project report.
- Search the knowledge base.
- Answer technical questions.

The BUXA Assistant serves as a digital project assistant rather than a decision-making system.

---

# AI Limitations

Artificial Intelligence shall never:

- Approve inspections
- Reject inspections
- Approve Quality Gates
- Close rectifications
- Modify inspection facts
- Alter original evidence
- Replace professional judgement

Every project decision remains the responsibility of the Flooring Project Engineer.

---

# Learning Strategy

Artificial Intelligence continuously improves by learning from completed projects.

Learning sources may include:

- Inspection Records
- Project Evidence
- Quality Standards
- Historical Projects
- Maintenance Records
- Customer Feedback

The objective is continuous improvement rather than autonomous decision-making.

---

# Human-Centred AI

BUXA PDMS™ adopts a human-centred approach to Artificial Intelligence.

Artificial Intelligence:

- Supports professionals
- Enhances productivity
- Reduces repetitive work
- Improves knowledge accessibility

People remain responsible for project quality.

---

# Trustworthy AI

Artificial Intelligence should always be:

Transparent

Traceable

Explainable

Reliable

Supportive

Users should always understand how AI-generated information was produced.

---

# Future Development

Future versions may support:

- Voice Assistant
- Natural Language Search
- Image Recognition
- Knowledge Graph
- Predictive Analytics
- Smart Scheduling
- Multi-language Assistant
- BIM Integration

without changing the fundamental AI philosophy.

---

# AI Maturity Model

The evolution of Artificial Intelligence within BUXA PDMS™ follows six maturity levels.

Level 1

Documentation Assistant

Automatically organises information and generates documents.

---

Level 2

Knowledge Assistant

Provides instant access to standards, procedures and historical knowledge.

---

Level 3

Project Assistant

Supports project planning, task management and workflow navigation.

---

Level 4

Risk Assistant

Identifies potential project risks and recommends further investigation.

---

Level 5

Project Intelligence

Analyses historical data to provide strategic insights and performance trends.

---

Level 6

Professional Judgement

Not supported.

Inspection approval, project acceptance and quality decisions remain the responsibility of qualified professionals.

---

# Single Source of Truth

Artificial Intelligence within BUXA PDMS™ exists to improve project delivery rather than replace professional expertise.

The system manages workflow.

Evidence represents reality.

Artificial Intelligence provides assistance.

The Flooring Project Engineer provides judgement.

Professional responsibility remains with people.
