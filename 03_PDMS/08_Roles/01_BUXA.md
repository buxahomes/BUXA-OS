# Purpose

This document defines the official role of BUXA within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

BUXA is the owner of the Project Delivery Management System and is responsible for establishing, maintaining and continuously improving the standards that govern every project delivered through BUXA PDMS™.

BUXA is more than a flooring supplier.

BUXA provides an integrated project delivery system that combines products, technical standards, quality management and professional project coordination.

---

# Vision

Exceptional flooring projects are not created by products alone.

They are created through a complete delivery system.

BUXA exists to ensure that every project is delivered consistently, professionally and predictably through the BUXA PDMS™ methodology.

---

# Role Definition

BUXA is the System Owner of the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

BUXA establishes the operating framework that enables every Flooring Project Engineer, Interior Designer and Installation Team to work within one unified project delivery methodology.

The purpose of BUXA is not simply to supply flooring products.

The purpose of BUXA is to deliver complete flooring projects.

Deliver outstanding projects.
