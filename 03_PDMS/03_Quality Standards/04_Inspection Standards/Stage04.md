# Purpose

This document defines the official Inspection Standards used during **Stage 04 — Installation Preparation** within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

These standards establish the inspection requirements used to verify that the project site is fully prepared before flooring installation begins.

Installation shall not commence until all applicable inspection standards have been successfully satisfied.

# Inspection Standards

---

# IS-031

## Inspection Item

现场施工条件确认

---

## Related Control Point

CP-031

---

## Inspection Objective

确认施工现场满足正式安装条件。

---

## Inspection Method

Site Inspection

---

## Inspection Tools

- Site Readiness Checklist
- Camera

---

## Inspection Location

Project Site

---

## Inspection Frequency

Before installation begins.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

The site shall be:

- Clean
- Accessible
- Safe
- Ready for installation

No activities shall interfere with flooring installation.

---

## Reference Standards

- BUXA PDMS™ Installation Standard
- Project Workflow

---

## Required Evidence

- Site photographs
- Site Readiness Checklist

---

## Inspection Record

Pass / Fail / N/A

---

## Non-Conformance Action

Installation shall not commence until site readiness has been confirmed.

---

## Notes

The Flooring Project Engineer is responsible for approving site readiness.

---

# IS-032

## Inspection Item

基层含水率检查

---

## Related Control Point

CP-032

---

## Inspection Objective

确认基层含水率满足产品安装要求。

---

## Inspection Method

Moisture Measurement

---

## Inspection Tools

- Digital Moisture Meter

---

## Inspection Location

Subfloor

---

## Inspection Frequency

Minimum five readings per room.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

Measured moisture content shall comply with the approved Product Passport and Installation Guide.

---

## Reference Standards

- Product Passport
- Installation Guide
- BUXA Technical Standards

---

## Required Evidence

- Moisture readings
- Photographs

---

## Inspection Record

Pass / Fail / N/A

---

## Non-Conformance Action

Installation shall not proceed until moisture levels comply with the required standard.

---

## Notes

Additional readings may be required in large areas or where moisture variation is suspected.

---

# IS-033

## Inspection Item

基层平整度检查

---

## Related Control Point

CP-033

---

## Inspection Objective

确认基层平整度符合安装要求。

---

## Inspection Method

Level Measurement

---

## Inspection Tools

- 2 m Straightedge
- Feeler Gauge

---

## Inspection Location

Subfloor

---

## Inspection Frequency

Representative measurements throughout the installation area.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

Subfloor flatness shall comply with the approved Installation Guide.

---

## Reference Standards

- Installation Guide
- Technical Standards

---

## Required Evidence

- Measurement Record
- Photographs

---

## Inspection Record

Pass / Fail / N/A

---

## Non-Conformance Action

Subfloor correction shall be completed before installation.

---

## Notes

Measurements should include areas near walls, transitions and doorways.

# Stage Summary

Stage 04 Inspection Standards verify that all technical, environmental and logistical requirements have been satisfied before flooring installation begins.

These standards ensure that installation proceeds only after the project site has been confirmed as fully prepared.

Successful completion of all applicable Inspection Standards enables Quality Gate 04 approval.
