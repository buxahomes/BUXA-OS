# Purpose

This document defines the official Inspection Standards used during **Stage 06 — Post-Installation Inspection** within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

These standards establish the inspection requirements used to verify that the completed flooring installation satisfies the approved design, technical requirements and BUXA PDMS™ Quality Standards.

Only projects that successfully complete these inspections may proceed to Project Handover.

# Stage Summary

Stage 06 Inspection Standards establish the professional verification requirements before project handover.

These standards verify:

- Overall installation quality
- Surface quality
- Joint quality
- Transition details
- Skirting installation
- Finished surface condition
- Defect rectification
- Inspection documentation
- Final quality verification

Successful completion of all applicable Inspection Standards enables Quality Gate 06 approval.
