# Purpose

This document defines the official Inspection Standards used during **Stage 08 — Aftercare** within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

These standards establish the requirements for post-handover customer support, long-term flooring performance, warranty coordination and continuous improvement.

The objective is to ensure that every completed project continues to deliver value throughout its service life while contributing to the continuous improvement of the BUXA PDMS™ system.

# Stage Summary

Stage 08 Inspection Standards establish the requirements for long-term customer support and continuous improvement.

These standards verify:

- Customer follow-up
- Product performance
- Maintenance support
- Aftercare issue management
- Warranty coordination
- Customer satisfaction
- Project review
- Knowledge capture
- Continuous improvement
- Project closure

Successful completion of these standards formally closes the project within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.
