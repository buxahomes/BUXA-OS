# Purpose

This document defines the official Inspection Standards used during **Stage 02 — Design Review** within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

These standards define how the approved design shall be technically reviewed before the project proceeds to Material Planning.

The objective is not to evaluate design creativity, but to verify delivery readiness.

# IS-011

## Inspection Item

设计图纸完整性审核

---

## Related Control Point

CP-011

---

## Inspection Objective

确认项目图纸完整，并满足地面系统技术审核要求。

---

## Inspection Method

Document Review

---

## Inspection Tools

- Floor Plan
- Elevations
- Detail Drawings
- Material Schedule

---

## Inspection Location

Project Documentation

---

## Inspection Frequency

Once before technical review.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

The following documents shall be available where applicable:

- Floor Plan
- Flooring Layout
- Detail Drawings
- Material Schedule
- Revision Version

---

## Required Evidence

Approved drawing package.

---

## Non-Conformance Action

Technical review shall not proceed until required drawings are available.

---

## Notes

Drawing revisions shall be clearly identified.

---

## Inspection Method

## Inspection Tools

- Design Drawings

---

## Inspection Location

---

## Inspection Frequency

Once before material planning.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Pattern

# Stage Summary

Stage 02 Inspection Standards establish the technical review requirements before material planning begins.

These standards verify:

- Drawing completeness
- Flooring layout
- Transition details
- Stair details
- Product compatibility
- Constructability
- Technical risks
- Design optimisation
- Delivery alignment

Successful completion of all applicable Inspection Standards enables Quality Gate 02 approval.
