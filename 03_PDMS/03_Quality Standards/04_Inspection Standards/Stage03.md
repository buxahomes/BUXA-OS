# Purpose

This document defines the official Inspection Standards used during **Stage 03 — Material Planning** within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery Management System.

These standards establish how flooring products, accessories, material quantities and delivery arrangements shall be verified before installation preparation begins.

The objective is to ensure that the project is fully prepared for successful installation.

# IS-021

## Inspection Item

地板产品确认

---

## Related Control Point

CP-021

---

## Inspection Objective

确认最终选定的地板产品符合设计方案及项目要求。

---

## Inspection Method

Document Review

---

## Inspection Tools

- Product Passport
- Product Selection Schedule
- Approved Drawings

---

## Inspection Location

Project Documentation

---

## Inspection Frequency

Once before material ordering.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

The approved flooring product shall match:

- Product Code
- Colour
- Surface Finish
- Dimensions
- Pattern

---

## Required Evidence

Approved Product Selection Record.

---

## Non-Conformance Action

Incorrect product selections shall be corrected before proceeding.

---

## Notes

Product selection shall reference the official Product Passport.
