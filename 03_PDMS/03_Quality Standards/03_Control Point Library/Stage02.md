# Purpose

This document defines all Control Points used during **Stage 02 — Design Review** within the BUXA PDMS™ Project Lifecycle.

The purpose of Design Review is to verify that the approved design can be successfully delivered through the BUXA PDMS™ methodology.

This stage focuses on technical feasibility, constructability and delivery readiness rather than design creativity.

# Stage Objective

The objective of Stage 02 is to ensure that the approved design can be implemented safely, accurately and efficiently.

Potential delivery risks should be identified before material planning begins.

# Stage Summary

Stage 02 verifies that the approved design can be delivered successfully through the BUXA PDMS™ Project Delivery System.

The Control Points within this stage focus on:

- Drawing completeness
- Technical feasibility
- Product compatibility
- Constructability
- Delivery readiness

Successful completion of these Control Points enables Quality Gate 02 approval.

Only approved projects proceed to Stage 03 — Material Planning.
