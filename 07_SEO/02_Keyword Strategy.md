# Purpose

This document defines how customer search queries connect with structured business knowledge within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Keywords are not the objective of search.

They are one representation of customer intent.

Customers search using words.

Business knowledge provides answers.

The purpose of keyword strategy is to ensure that every important customer question can be answered through structured business knowledge.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to ensure that every important customer question can be answered accurately, consistently and confidently.

Whether customers search using keywords, natural language, voice, Artificial Intelligence or future search technologies, they should always be connected to the same structured business knowledge.

Search should lead customers to understanding.

Understanding should build confidence.

Confidence should support better decisions.

---

# Keyword Philosophy

Keywords are entry points.

Business knowledge is the destination.

Search queries may change.

Business knowledge should remain consistent.

Keyword optimisation should never compromise knowledge quality.

Understanding customer intent is more valuable than targeting individual keywords.

# Knowledge Coverage Model

The objective of keyword strategy is not to rank for every keyword.

The objective is to ensure that every important customer question can be answered through structured business knowledge.

```text
Customer Questions
        │
        ▼
Knowledge Coverage
        │
        ▼
Knowledge Quality
        │
        ▼
Customer Confidence
```

Every unanswered customer question represents a knowledge gap.

Every knowledge gap represents an opportunity to improve the Business Operating System.

Keyword research therefore becomes knowledge research.

# Content Relationship

Business knowledge determines content.

Content improves discoverability.

Keywords improve accessibility.

The objective is not to write for algorithms.

The objective is to answer customer questions accurately.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence supports search optimisation by:

- Understanding customer intent
- Analysing search behaviour
- Identifying knowledge gaps
- Recommending content opportunities
- Strengthening entity relationships
- Improving semantic understanding

Artificial Intelligence should optimise discoverability rather than keyword density.
