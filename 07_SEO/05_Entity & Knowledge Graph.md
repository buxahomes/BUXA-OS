# Purpose

This document defines how business entities and their relationships are organised within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Modern search technologies increasingly understand entities rather than isolated keywords.

The objective is to organise business knowledge into a connected knowledge graph that improves discoverability, understanding and customer confidence.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build a business knowledge graph that accurately represents every important concept within the organisation.

Business entities should be consistently defined, clearly related and understandable by both people and Artificial Intelligence.

Knowledge should exist as an interconnected system rather than isolated pages.

---

# Entity Philosophy

Entities are the building blocks of business knowledge.

Relationships create understanding.

Knowledge graphs organise relationships.

Understanding builds confidence.

Business knowledge should always be organised around meaningful concepts rather than isolated keywords.

---

# Knowledge Graph

The BUXA Knowledge Graph represents the relationships between all business entities.

The objective is to ensure that every important business concept is connected with relevant knowledge.

Examples include:

- Products ↔ Installation
- Products ↔ Design Resources
- Products ↔ Warranty
- PDMS ↔ Inspection Reports
- Inspection Reports ↔ Quality Standards
- Quality Standards ↔ Customer Experience

Knowledge becomes more valuable as relationships become stronger.

---

# Entity Standards

Every business entity should maintain:

- One official name
- One official definition
- Consistent terminology
- Clear business purpose
- Related entities
- Related knowledge

Each entity should exist only once within the Business Operating System.

---

# Relationship Principles

Relationships should always be:

- Logical
- Consistent
- Evidence-based
- Easy to understand
- Scalable

Business knowledge should behave as a connected ecosystem.

- Detecting missing relationships
- Recommending knowledge connections

Artificial Intelligence should understand business concepts rather than individual keywords.
