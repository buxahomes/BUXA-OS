# Purpose

This document defines how search intent is understood within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Search intent is not defined by keywords alone.

It is defined by the uncertainty customers are trying to reduce.

Understanding search intent allows BUXA to deliver the right business knowledge at the right stage of the customer confidence journey.

---

# Vision

Every customer searches for knowledge because they are trying to answer a question or reduce uncertainty.

The objective of search is not simply to provide information.

The objective is to help customers build confidence and make better decisions.

Search intent should always be interpreted through customer needs rather than search terms.

---

# Search Philosophy

Customers do not search because they want keywords.

Customers search because they need confidence.

Every search begins with uncertainty.

Knowledge removes uncertainty.

Confidence supports decisions.

Understanding customer intent is more valuable than understanding search volume.

---

# Search Intent Architecture

Every search follows one consistent framework.

```text
Customer Uncertainty
        │
        ▼
Search Intent
        │
        ▼
Business Knowledge
        │
        ▼
Knowledge Understanding
        │
        ▼
Customer Confidence
        │
        ▼
Decision
```

Search should continuously reduce uncertainty through structured business knowledge.

# Search Intent Categories

The BUXA Search System recognises five primary search intentions.

---

## Discover

Customer Question

What is available?

Examples

- Timber flooring ideas
- Oak flooring
- Interior inspiration

Marketing Goal

Introduce possibilities.

---

## Understand

Customer Question

How does this work?

Examples

- Engineered flooring
- Flooring thickness
- Installation methods
- Herringbone flooring

Marketing Goal

Provide knowledge.

---

## Compare

Customer Question

Which option is better?

Examples

- Engineered vs solid timber
- Oak colour comparison
- Flooring brands

Marketing Goal

Explain differences objectively.

---

## Validate

Customer Question

Can I trust this decision?

Examples

- Reviews
- Certifications
- Warranty
- PDMS
- Project quality

Marketing Goal

Strengthen confidence.

---

## Decide

Customer Question

What should I do next?

Examples

- Sample Box
- Consultation
- Contact
- Product selection

Marketing Goal

Support confident decisions.

# Search Intent Mapping

Every search intent requires different business knowledge.

| Search Intent | Primary Knowledge |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Discover | Inspiration, Collections |
| Understand | Guides, Articles, FAQs |
| Compare | Product Comparison, Case Studies |
| Validate | Quality Assurance, PDMS, Warranty |
| Decide | Sample Box, Contact, Consultation |

Marketing should always provide the most relevant knowledge.

# Confidence Mapping

Different search intentions require different confidence.

| Search Intent | Confidence Goal |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Discover | Curiosity |
| Understand | Knowledge Confidence |
| Compare | Product Confidence |
| Validate | Trust Confidence |
| Decide | Decision Confidence |

Search should strengthen confidence progressively.
