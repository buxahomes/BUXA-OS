# Purpose

This module defines how product information is organised, maintained and governed across the entire BUXA ecosystem.

Every product should have one official source of truth.

Product information must never be duplicated across websites, social media, AI prompts, marketing materials or internal documents.

This module establishes the product management standards for all current and future BUXA product categories.

---

# Product Management Principles

## Single Source of Truth

Each product has one official product document.

All public-facing content should reference this document.

No duplicate product documentation should exist.

---

## Global Product Identity

Every product has one global product identity.

Each product is assigned one permanent product code.

Example:

HD08

A product code should never change once published.

Retired product codes remain reserved.

---

## Content Before Channel

Products are created before marketing channels.

Websites, social media, AI systems, catalogues and advertising should all reference the same product information.

Channels do not create product information.

They consume it.

---

## Products Are Global

BUXA products are managed globally.

The same product identity should be used across different countries whenever possible.

Regional sales strategies should not create duplicate products.

---

## Sales Models Are Local

Products remain global.

Sales models are regional.

Example:

Australia

Website

Google Ads

Sample Box

Door-to-door Delivery

China

Social Media

Designer Channels

Showroom

BUXA PDMS™

Project Delivery

Different sales models do not require different product identities.

---

## One Product One File

Each product has one dedicated document.

Example

HD08.md

The product document becomes the official source for:

- Product Story
- Technical Specifications
- Colour
- Texture
- Construction
- Images
- Applications
- Design Style
- Marketing Assets

---

## Product Lifecycle

Every product follows one lifecycle.

Planning

↓

Development

↓

Active

↓

Discontinued

Retired products remain archived.

Product codes are never reused.

---

# Current Product Content Strategy

At the current stage, the official BUXA website product range serves as the primary content baseline.

The current HD product range is used for:

- Website
- Facebook
- Instagram
- Pinterest
- YouTube
- Xiaohongshu
- AI-generated content
- Marketing materials

This ensures consistent brand communication across all markets.

---

## China Showroom Products

China showroom products may differ from the current website product range.

These differences are managed separately.

They do not automatically change the official product catalogue.

The showroom exists primarily to:

- Support designer channels
- Support offline product selection
- Support project delivery
- Build long-term designer relationships

Products displayed in the showroom should only be added to the official product library after formal approval.

---

## Future Product Expansion

As new products are introduced:

- New product codes are assigned sequentially.
- Existing product codes remain unchanged.
- Product documentation is created before public release.
- Marketing assets are generated from the official product document.

# Folder Responsibilities

## README.md

Defines product management principles.

---

## 00_Product Catalog.md

Maintains the official list of active products.

No technical specifications should be stored here.

---

## 01_Flooring

Contains one document for each flooring product.

One product.

One file.

---

## 02_Collections

Defines product collections.

Examples include:

- Sample Collections
- Designer Collections
- Future Product Series

Collections organise products.

They do not replace products.

---

## 03_Accessories

Contains all accessory products.

Examples:

- Skirting Boards
- Stair Nosing
- T-Mould
- Reducers
- End Caps

Accessories follow the same documentation principles as flooring products.

---

## 04_Specifications

Contains technical specifications shared across multiple products.

Examples:

- Construction Standards
- Surface Finishes
- Dimensions
- Installation Standards
- Warranty Standards

Common specifications should be referenced rather than duplicated.

---

## 05_Discontinued

Stores retired products.

Historical information is preserved.

Product codes remain reserved.

---

## 06_Showroom

Contains internal showroom information.

This folder supports:

- Designer presentations
- Offline sales
- Sample displays
- Regional showroom planning

It is not considered the official public product catalogue.

# Product Governance

Every product must have:

- One official product code
- One official product document
- One approved product name
- One approved image library
- One approved specification
- One approval record

No public content should be created before the official product documentation is completed.
