# 2. Product Overview

Midnight is a premium European Oak engineered timber flooring featuring a deep charcoal-brown tone designed for contemporary architectural interiors.

Its rich colour depth enhances contrast, highlights natural oak grain and creates a sophisticated foundation that complements premium materials such as natural stone, metal, glass and textured fabrics.

Rather than making a space feel darker, Midnight adds visual definition and architectural presence while maintaining the warmth and authenticity of genuine European Oak.

---

## Signature Statement

Bold depth, timeless architecture.

---

# 3. Product Story

Inspired by the quiet atmosphere of evening architecture and the richness of natural timber after sunset, Midnight explores depth rather than darkness.

Its carefully balanced charcoal-brown tone was developed to create visual contrast without losing the warmth inherent in European Oak.

The result is a floor that feels refined, confident and enduring, allowing architecture, lighting and carefully selected materials to become even more expressive.
