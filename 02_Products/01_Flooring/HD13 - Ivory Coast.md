# 2. Product Overview

Ivory Coast is a premium European Oak engineered timber flooring featuring a soft ivory tone inspired by natural limestone, fine linen and contemporary coastal architecture.

Its subtle colour variation, authentic European Oak grain and refined hand-brushed matte finish create interiors that feel calm, sophisticated and effortlessly welcoming.

Designed for modern residential living, Ivory Coast provides a soft architectural foundation that complements both minimalist and richly layered interiors.

---

## Signature Statement

Soft elegance, naturally enduring.

---

# 3. Product Story

Inspired by the quiet beauty of ivory-toned natural materials, Ivory Coast celebrates softness without sacrificing character.

Rather than pursuing stark white finishes, its carefully balanced tone preserves the warmth and authenticity of European Oak while introducing a light, refined atmosphere.

The result is a floor that feels elegant, timeless and quietly luxurious, allowing natural materials, architectural forms and soft lighting to work together harmoniously.
