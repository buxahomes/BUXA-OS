# 2. Product Overview

Coastal Dune is a premium European Oak engineered timber flooring inspired by the quiet elegance of Australia's coastal landscapes.

Its refined coastal beige tone combines the warmth of natural sand with the subtle sophistication of contemporary architectural interiors, creating spaces that feel bright, balanced and effortlessly luxurious.

Designed for modern homes where natural materials and timeless design are valued, Coastal Dune offers exceptional versatility while maintaining a distinctive coastal character.

---

## Signature Statement

Coastal elegance, beautifully enduring.

---

# 3. Product Story

Inspired by the rolling coastal dunes and warm natural light of Australia's shoreline, Coastal Dune captures the relaxed sophistication of premium coastal living.

Its carefully balanced beige tone preserves the authenticity of European Oak while introducing a soft architectural warmth that complements natural stone, linen, textured plaster and contemporary interiors.

The result is a flooring that feels relaxed yet refined, offering a timeless foundation for homes designed around light, space and natural materials.
