# 2. Product Overview

Amber Haven is a premium European Oak engineered timber flooring featuring a rich warm brown tone inspired by natural amber and timeless architectural materials.

Its balanced colour depth, refined hand-brushed texture and authentic oak grain create interiors that feel welcoming, sophisticated and enduring.

Amber Haven is designed for homes seeking warmth, elegance and a stronger visual presence without overwhelming the overall interior design.

---

# 3. Product Story

Inspired by the warmth of natural amber and handcrafted timber, Amber Haven celebrates the enduring beauty of authentic European Oak.

Rather than relying on bold colour contrasts, the design focuses on depth, richness and natural variation, creating interiors that feel calm, inviting and timeless.

Its warm character works equally well in modern architectural spaces and classic residential environments, allowing designers to create interiors with lasting visual appeal.
