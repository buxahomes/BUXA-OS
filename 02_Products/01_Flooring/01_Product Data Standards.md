# Product Data Standards

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Owner: Founder

Review Cycle: Every 12 Months

Document Type: Product Data Dictionary

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official data standards for all BUXA flooring products.

It establishes the approved values, terminology and data structure used throughout the Product Layer.

Every Product Passport must follow these standards.

This document ensures consistency across:

- Product Passports
- Website
- Product Catalogue
- AI Systems
- Marketing
- SEO
- Sales Materials
- Future Automation

---

# Data Standard Principles

## Standardised Data

Every product attribute must use approved terminology.

Avoid creating new values unless officially approved.

---

## Reusable Data

The same data should be reused across all products whenever possible.

---

## AI Readability

All values should be easy for AI systems to understand and reuse.

---

## Long-term Scalability

New values may be added.

Existing approved values should not be modified without review.
