# 2. Product Overview

HD01 – Pure Mist is a light-toned European Oak engineered timber floor designed for contemporary Australian homes.

Its balanced warm undertone, natural European oak grain and refined hand-brushed surface create a calm and timeless appearance without feeling overly white or grey.

Pure Mist is one of the most versatile colours within the BUXA collection, making it suitable for a wide range of architectural styles from Modern Coastal to Scandinavian interiors.

---

# 3. Product Story

Pure Mist was inspired by the soft natural colours found in Australia's coastal landscape.

Rather than creating a flooring colour that follows short-term trends, the design focuses on timeless warmth, natural oak character and long-term livability.

Its subtle variation in grain and texture allows furniture, lighting and architectural elements to become the visual focus while maintaining a sophisticated natural foundation.
