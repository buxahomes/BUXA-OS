# 2. Product Overview

Golden Vale is a premium European Oak engineered timber flooring featuring a naturally warm golden tone inspired by Australian sunlight and open landscapes.

Its balanced colour, authentic oak grain and refined hand-brushed matte finish create interiors that feel welcoming, comfortable and full of natural warmth.

Golden Vale is designed for homeowners and designers seeking a timeless timber floor with warmth, character and everyday versatility.

## Product Design Statement

Warm natural light captured in European Oak.

---

# 3. Product Story

Inspired by the golden light that defines the Australian landscape, Golden Vale celebrates warmth, openness and natural living.

Rather than creating dramatic contrast, the design focuses on balance and authenticity, allowing the natural beauty of European Oak to become part of everyday life.

Its welcoming appearance creates interiors that remain comfortable, timeless and effortlessly elegant.
