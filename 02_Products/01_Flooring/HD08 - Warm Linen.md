# 2. Product Overview

Warm Linen is a refined European Oak engineered timber flooring featuring a soft warm-neutral tone inspired by natural linen, layered textiles and understated contemporary interiors.

Its balanced colour avoids appearing overly yellow, grey or dark, allowing the natural European Oak grain and hand-brushed texture to remain visible without dominating the space.

Warm Linen creates interiors that feel calm, inviting and quietly sophisticated, while remaining versatile enough for family homes, apartments, designer residences and hospitality-inspired environments.

---

## Signature Statement

Quiet warmth, naturally refined.

---

# 3. Product Story

Inspired by the softness and understated elegance of natural linen, Warm Linen was developed to bring gentle warmth and visual balance to contemporary interiors.

Rather than relying on strong contrast or dramatic colour, the design focuses on restraint, texture and subtle tonal variation. Its natural oak grain introduces character, while the warm-neutral palette allows furniture, stone, textiles and architectural details to work together harmoniously.

The result is a floor that feels comfortable and refined, creating a calm foundation for both relaxed residential living and more polished design expressions.
