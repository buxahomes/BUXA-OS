# 2. Product Overview

Sand Drift is a premium European Oak engineered timber flooring inspired by the soft colours of coastal sand and natural Australian landscapes.

Its light sandy beige tone, authentic European oak grain and refined hand-brushed matte finish create interiors that feel open, relaxed and filled with natural light.

Sand Drift is particularly suited to contemporary coastal homes, designer residences and modern interiors seeking warmth without excessive colour contrast.

## Product Design Statement

Relaxed coastal living, naturally expressed.

---

# 3. Product Story

Inspired by windswept coastal dunes and the quiet beauty of Australia's shoreline, Sand Drift captures the subtle warmth and softness of natural sand.

Rather than creating a strong visual statement, the design provides a calm architectural foundation that allows furniture, artwork and natural light to become the focal points of the space.

Its understated character makes it a timeless flooring choice for both contemporary and classic interiors.
