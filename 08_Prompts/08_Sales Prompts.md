# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence retrieves and applies official sales standards within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Sales prompts do not replace professional salespeople.

They retrieve structured business knowledge and apply official sales principles to support customer decision-making.

The purpose of sales prompts is to ensure that every customer receives accurate, consistent and trustworthy guidance throughout the decision process.

---

# Vision

Every AI-assisted sales interaction should strengthen customer confidence rather than increase sales pressure.

Customers should receive professional guidance based on official business knowledge.

Sales prompts should support informed decisions and long-term customer relationships.

---

# Sales Philosophy

Customers do not need persuasion.

Customers need confidence.

Sales retrieves business knowledge.

Artificial Intelligence organises that knowledge.

Customers make informed decisions.

Long-term trust is more valuable than short-term sales.

# Sales Responsibilities

Sales prompts should ensure that Artificial Intelligence:

- Understands customer needs.
- Retrieves official business knowledge.
- Explains products objectively.
- Recommends appropriate solutions.
- Clarifies limitations where necessary.
- Reduces customer uncertainty.
- Strengthens long-term trust.

Sales prompts support decisions rather than pressure customers.

# Sales Context Assembly

Sales guidance should always be generated from complete customer context.

Sales context may include:

- Customer Goals
- Project Type
- Design Style
- Product Requirements
- Budget Considerations
- Project Timeline
- Installation Requirements
- Previous Customer Questions
- Relevant Website Resources
- Related Product Knowledge

Better customer context produces better recommendations.

---

# Decision Readiness

Before making any recommendation, Artificial Intelligence should determine the customer's decision readiness.

Examples include:

- Exploring options
- Learning about products
- Comparing alternatives
- Validating a preferred solution
- Ready to proceed

Different levels of decision readiness require different guidance.

Artificial Intelligence should provide only the knowledge needed for the customer's current stage.

The objective is to support confident decisions rather than accelerate the sales process.

---

# Sales Validation

Before generating any recommendation, Artificial Intelligence should confirm that:

- Official business knowledge is being used.
- Customer requirements are clearly understood.
- Product recommendations are appropriate.
- Limitations have been explained where necessary.
- Customer confidence is strengthened.
- Recommendations remain consistent with the Business Operating System.

Sales validation should occur before recommendations are presented.

---

# Sales Principles

Every sales prompt should:

- Retrieve official business knowledge.
- Understand customer objectives.
- Recommend appropriate solutions.
- Explain recommendations clearly.
- Maintain brand consistency.
- Strengthen customer confidence.
- Support long-term relationships.

Artificial Intelligence should never:

- Pressure customers into decisions.
- Exaggerate product capabilities.
- Recommend unsuitable products.
- Invent business information.
- Prioritise sales over customer value.
