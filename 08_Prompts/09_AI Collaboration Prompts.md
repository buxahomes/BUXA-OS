Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Owner: Founder

Last Updated: July 2026

Document Type: Prompt Standard

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence coordinates multiple business capabilities within the BUXA Business Operating System.

AI collaboration prompts do not replace individual prompt standards.

They orchestrate multiple prompt capabilities to solve business tasks that require knowledge from more than one business module.

The purpose of AI collaboration prompts is to ensure that complex business activities remain consistent, efficient and aligned with the Business Operating System.

---

# Vision

Artificial Intelligence should collaborate across business capabilities rather than operate as isolated task generators.

Every complex business task should retrieve only the knowledge required for that objective.

Business knowledge should remain modular.

Artificial Intelligence should coordinate its application.

---

# Collaboration Philosophy

Business knowledge is modular.

Business capabilities are specialised.

Artificial Intelligence coordinates capabilities.

Business outputs remain unified.

Business learning improves future collaboration.

Collaboration should increase consistency rather than complexity.

---

# Collaboration Architecture

Every collaborative AI task follows one consistent framework.

```text
Business Objective
        │
        ▼
Capability Selection
        │
        ▼
Context Assembly
        │
        ▼
Artificial Intelligence
        │
        ▼
Business Output
        │
        ▼
Business Learning
```

Artificial Intelligence coordinates business capabilities rather than replacing them.

---

# Capability Selection

Artificial Intelligence should first identify which business capabilities are required.

Examples include:

| Business Task | Required Capabilities |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Product Page | Brand + Product + Website + SEO |
| Buying Guide | Product + Website + Marketing + SEO |
| Social Media | Brand + Marketing + Product |
| Google Ads | Brand + Marketing + SEO |
| Designer Proposal | Brand + Product + PDMS + Sales |
| Customer Consultation | Brand + Product + Sales |
| Inspection Summary | PDMS + Product |
| FAQ Creation | Product + Website + SEO |

Only the required capabilities should be retrieved.

---

# Capability Composition

Business capabilities should be composed dynamically according to the business objective.

Artificial Intelligence should avoid loading unnecessary knowledge.

Examples include:

Simple Tasks

↓

Single Capability

Moderate Tasks

↓

Two to Four Capabilities

Complex Tasks

↓

Cross-module Capability Composition

The objective is to retrieve the right knowledge rather than all available knowledge.

Smaller, well-targeted context improves both quality and efficiency.

---

# Context Assembly

After selecting the required capabilities, Artificial Intelligence should assemble complete business context.

Context may include:

- Business Objectives
- Brand Standards
- Product Knowledge
- Website Standards
- Marketing Strategy
- Search Standards
- PDMS Standards
- Customer Context
- Project Context
- Previous Business Learning

Complete context produces higher-quality business outputs.

---

# Collaboration Responsibilities

AI collaboration prompts should ensure that Artificial Intelligence:

- Selects the correct business capabilities.
- Retrieves official business knowledge.
- Preserves terminology.
- Maintains consistency.
- Applies official business standards.
- Supports customer confidence.
- Generates business learning.

Collaboration should improve business quality rather than increase output volume.

---

# Collaboration Validation

Before generating any collaborative output, Artificial Intelligence should confirm that:

- All required capabilities have been included.
- Official business knowledge has been retrieved.
- Business terminology is consistent.
- Responsibilities remain clear.
- Customer confidence is strengthened.
- Human review remains possible.

Collaboration should strengthen business consistency.

---

# Collaboration Principles

Every collaboration prompt should:

- Start with the business objective.
- Retrieve only the required capabilities.
- Preserve concept ownership.
- Avoid duplicate knowledge.
- Maintain consistent terminology.
- Strengthen customer confidence.
- Contribute to business learning.

Artificial Intelligence should never:

- Mix conflicting business standards.
- Invent missing knowledge.
- Ignore official terminology.
- Replace human business judgement.
