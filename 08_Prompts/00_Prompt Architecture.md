Status: Approved

Owner: Founder

Last Updated: July 2026

Document Type: System Architecture

---

# Purpose

This document defines the architecture of the Prompt System within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Prompts are not standalone instructions.

They are structured knowledge interfaces that enable Artificial Intelligence to retrieve, understand and apply official business knowledge.

The objective is to ensure that every AI interaction is consistent, traceable and aligned with the Business Operating System.

---

# Vision

The Prompt System should provide a unified interface between business knowledge and Artificial Intelligence.

Regardless of which AI platform is used, prompts should consistently invoke the same business knowledge and business standards.

Artificial Intelligence should always operate from structured knowledge rather than isolated instructions.

---

# Prompt Philosophy

Business knowledge remains the foundation.

Prompts invoke knowledge.

Artificial Intelligence applies knowledge.

Business outputs create evidence.

Evidence improves business knowledge.

Prompts should never become an alternative knowledge base.

They should remain interfaces to the Business Operating System.

---

# Prompt Architecture

Every AI interaction follows one consistent architecture.

```text
Business Knowledge
        │
        ▼
Knowledge Interface
        │
        ▼
Context Assembly
        │
        ▼
Artificial Intelligence
        │
        ▼
Business Output
        │
        ▼

```

Prompt architecture connects business knowledge with AI execution.

---

# Prompt Layers

The Prompt System consists of six logical layers.

---

## Layer 01

### Business Knowledge

Official business knowledge originates from:

- Brand
- Products
- PDMS
- Website
- Marketing
- SEO

Business knowledge remains the single source of truth.

---

## Layer 02

### Knowledge Interface

Prompts define:

- Business objective
- Required knowledge
- Business context
- Expected behaviour

The interface determines what knowledge Artificial Intelligence should access.

---

## Layer 03

### Context Assembly

Relevant business knowledge is assembled before AI execution.

Examples include:

- Product standards
- Brand terminology
- PDMS procedures
- Website standards
- Marketing principles

Context should always originate from official business knowledge.

---

## Layer 04

### Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence should:

- Understand context
- Retrieve knowledge
- Apply business standards
- Produce useful outputs

Artificial Intelligence does not replace business knowledge.

---

## Layer 05

### Business Output

Outputs may include:

- Marketing content
- Website content
- Product documentation
- Inspection reports
- Sales materials
- Customer support

Every output should remain consistent with official business knowledge.

---

## Layer 06

### Business Learning

Every AI interaction generates evidence.

Examples include:

- Better prompts
- Customer questions
- Missing knowledge
- Improved terminology
- AI recommendations

Learning continuously strengthens the Business Operating System.

---

```text
Business Objective
        │
        ▼

        │
        ▼
Context Assembly
        │
        ▼
AI Execution
        │
        ▼
Human Review
        │
        ▼

```

Prompts should improve consistency rather than replace professional judgement.

---

# Prompt Principles

Every prompt should:

- Retrieve official business knowledge
- Preserve terminology
- Follow business standards
- Explain rather than invent
- Maintain consistency
- Support customer confidence
- Generate business learning

Prompts should never define business strategy.

---

# Prompt Standardisation

Every prompt should clearly define:

- Purpose
- Required knowledge
- Context
- Business rules
- Expected output
- Human responsibilities

A standard structure improves scalability and consistency.

# Artificial Intelligence

- Understanding business context
- Maintaining consistency
