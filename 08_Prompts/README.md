# Purpose

This module defines how structured business knowledge is invoked, interpreted and applied by Artificial Intelligence within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Prompts are not simply instructions for Artificial Intelligence.

They are structured interfaces between the Business Operating System and Artificial Intelligence.

The purpose of this module is to ensure that every AI interaction is based on official business knowledge, consistent business standards and clearly defined responsibilities.

Artificial Intelligence should never operate independently of the Business Operating System.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build an AI-native business where every AI capability operates from the same structured knowledge foundation.

Prompts should provide consistent access to business knowledge regardless of which Artificial Intelligence platform is being used.

As AI technologies evolve, the knowledge interface should remain stable.

Business knowledge remains the foundation.

Prompts provide the interface.

Artificial Intelligence provides the execution.

---

# Prompt Philosophy

Business knowledge should never be rewritten for every AI interaction.

Business knowledge should be invoked through structured knowledge interfaces.

Prompts should retrieve knowledge rather than replace knowledge.

Every prompt should strengthen consistency.

Every AI interaction should reinforce the Business Operating System.

---

# Core Objectives

The Prompt System supports six primary objectives.

## Consistent Knowledge Invocation

Every AI interaction should retrieve knowledge from the same official business source.

---

## Standardised AI Behaviour

Artificial Intelligence should follow consistent business standards regardless of task.

---

## Knowledge Reuse

Business knowledge should be created once and reused across every AI interaction.

---

## Business Consistency

Prompts should preserve:

- Brand standards
- Product knowledge
- PDMS standards
- Website standards
- Marketing standards
- SEO standards

---

## Human Responsibility

Artificial Intelligence supports execution.

People remain responsible for business decisions.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Prompt performance should continuously improve through the Business Learning Loop.
