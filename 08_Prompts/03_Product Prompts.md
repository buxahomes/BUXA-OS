Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Owner: Founder

Last Updated: July 2026

Document Type: Prompt Standard

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence retrieves and applies official product knowledge within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Product prompts do not define products.

They retrieve structured product knowledge from the official Product module and apply it consistently across different business scenarios.

The purpose of product prompts is to ensure that every AI-generated response reflects accurate, consistent and officially approved product knowledge.

---

# Vision

Every AI-generated product response should accurately represent the official BUXA product portfolio.

Regardless of the communication channel or AI platform, customers should receive the same reliable product knowledge.

Product prompts should reduce uncertainty and strengthen customer confidence.

---

# Product Philosophy

Products are defined by official business knowledge.

Artificial Intelligence should never invent product information.

Product prompts retrieve official product knowledge.

Artificial Intelligence explains that knowledge according to customer needs.

Consistency builds confidence.

---

# Product Knowledge

Product prompts should retrieve information from official product sources, including:

- Product Passports
- Product Specifications
- Collections
- Product Features
- Installation Requirements
- Care & Maintenance
- Warranty
- FAQs
- Quality Standards
- Sustainability Information

The Product module remains the single source of truth.

---

# Product Responsibilities

Product prompts should ensure that Artificial Intelligence:

- Uses official product terminology.
- Explains product features accurately.
- Preserves technical specifications.
- Recommends appropriate supporting resources.
- Avoids unsupported claims.
- Strengthens customer confidence.

Product prompts should always prioritise accuracy over persuasion.

---

# Product Validation

Before generating any response, Artificial Intelligence should confirm that:

- Product information is accurate.
- Specifications match the official Product Passport.
- Terminology follows official standards.
- Recommendations remain consistent with the Product module.
- Any limitations or conditions are clearly explained.

Product validation should occur before response generation.

---

# Product Knowledge Completeness

Artificial Intelligence should retrieve complete product knowledge rather than isolated specifications.

Product knowledge should include:

- Product Identity
- Technical Specifications
- Product Features
- Design Intent
- Installation Requirements
- Care & Maintenance
- Warranty
- Related Products
- Supporting Resources

Customers should receive complete product understanding rather than fragmented information.

Complete knowledge reduces uncertainty and strengthens confidence.

---

# Product Principles

Every product prompt should:

- Retrieve official product knowledge.
- Preserve technical accuracy.
- Use consistent terminology.
- Explain products clearly.
- Recommend related product knowledge when appropriate.
- Strengthen customer confidence.

Artificial Intelligence should never:

- Invent specifications.
- Guess unavailable information.
- Recommend unsuitable products.
- Modify official product terminology.
