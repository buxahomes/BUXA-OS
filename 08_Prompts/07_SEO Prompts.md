# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence retrieves and applies official Search Standards within the BUXA Business Operating System.

SEO prompts do not optimise search engines.

They retrieve structured business knowledge and apply the official Search System to improve knowledge discoverability across traditional search engines, AI search platforms and future knowledge discovery technologies.

The purpose of SEO prompts is to ensure that business knowledge remains discoverable, understandable and trustworthy regardless of how customers search.

---

# Vision

Every AI-generated content asset should improve the discoverability of structured business knowledge.

Search technologies will continue to evolve.

Business knowledge should remain consistently discoverable across Search Engine Optimisation (SEO), Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO), Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) and future AI-native search systems.

SEO prompts should optimise knowledge rather than individual search technologies.

---

# Search Philosophy

Business knowledge already exists.

Search organises discoverability.

SEO prompts retrieve official search standards.

Artificial Intelligence improves discoverability.

Customer understanding builds confidence.

Business learning continuously improves discoverability.

# Search Context Assembly

Search optimisation should always begin with complete search context.

Search context may include:

- Customer Questions
- Search Intent
- Target Audience
- Business Knowledge
- Website Structure
- Entity Relationships
- Related Knowledge
- Internal Linking
- Knowledge Coverage
- Previous Search Insights

Better context produces better discoverability.

---

# Search Objective

Before performing any search optimisation, Artificial Intelligence should identify the primary objective of the task.

Examples include:

- Improve Knowledge Discoverability
- Answer Customer Questions
- Strengthen Entity Authority
- Expand Knowledge Coverage
- Improve Semantic Relationships
- Support AI Understanding

Search objectives should always prioritise customer understanding over algorithm optimisation.

Knowledge discoverability remains the primary objective of the Search System.

---

# Search Validation

Before generating any search-optimised output, Artificial Intelligence should confirm that:

- Official search standards are being followed.
- Business knowledge is accurate.
- Customer questions are fully answered.
- Entity relationships remain consistent.
- Internal knowledge connections are maintained.
- Customer confidence is strengthened.

Search validation should occur before publication.

# Search Principles

Every SEO prompt should:

- Retrieve official search knowledge.
- Improve discoverability.
- Preserve entity relationships.
- Strengthen semantic consistency.
- Answer customer questions completely.
- Improve customer understanding.
- Contribute to business learning.

- Optimise for algorithms alone.
