# Purpose

This document defines the search architecture of the BUXA digital ecosystem.

Search Engine Optimisation is not treated as a marketing tactic.

It is the process of making structured business knowledge discoverable, understandable and trustworthy for both search engines and users.

The objective is sustainable visibility through authoritative content.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to become the most trusted source of engineered timber flooring knowledge.

Search visibility is earned by publishing accurate, structured and valuable information.

SEO should reflect business expertise rather than search manipulation.

---

# SEO Philosophy

Search begins with customer questions.

SEO exists to provide the best answer.

Not the most optimised page.

Knowledge creates authority.

Authority creates visibility.

Visibility creates business growth.

---

# Core Principles

## User Intent First

Every page should satisfy a real customer search intent.

Examples include:

- Learn
- Compare
- Evaluate
- Solve
- Purchase

Search intent should determine page content.

---

## Knowledge Before Keywords

Keywords help users discover information.

Knowledge helps users trust information.

Keyword optimisation should never reduce content quality.

---

## One Topic

One Page

Every important search topic should have one authoritative page.

Avoid duplicate content.

Avoid competing pages.

---

## Topical Authority

Content should be organised into complete knowledge clusters.

Authority is built by covering an entire subject rather than isolated keywords.

---

## Continuous Improvement

SEO performance improves as business knowledge grows.

Every new content asset strengthens the entire website.

---

# Search Intent Architecture

The website supports four primary search intentions.

## Informational

Customer objective:

Learn.

Examples:

- What is engineered flooring?
- How to choose timber flooring?
- Flooring maintenance

Typical pages:

- Guides
- Blog Articles
- FAQs

---

## Commercial Investigation

Customer objective:

Compare options.

Examples:

- European Oak Flooring
- Best Timber Flooring
- Flooring Colours

Typical pages:

- Collection Pages
- Buying Guides
- Comparison Pages

---

## Transactional

Customer objective:

Take action.

Examples:

- Order Sample Box
- Contact
- Packages

Typical pages:

- Product Pages
- Sample Box
- Contact

---

## Navigational

Customer objective:

Find BUXA.

Examples:

- BUXA
- BUXA Flooring
- BUXA Sample Box

These searches should always lead to official pages.

---

# SEO Content Structure

Each topic should form a structured content cluster.

Example:

```text
Engineered Timber Flooring

↓

Buying Guide

↓

Collections

↓

Individual Products

↓

Installation Guide

↓

Maintenance Guide

↓

FAQs
```

The objective is complete topic coverage.

---

# Internal Linking

Internal links should reflect knowledge relationships.

Every important page should connect naturally to:

- Related Products
- Related Guides
- Related Collections
- Related Resources
- Related FAQs

Internal links improve navigation for both users and search engines.

---

# Structured Content

Every page should include clearly defined:

- Title
- Heading hierarchy
- Summary
- Supporting evidence
- Related knowledge
- Call to Action

Structured information improves discoverability.

---

# Trust Signals

Search engines evaluate trust through evidence.

Examples include:

- Original content
- Technical expertise
- Quality standards
- Certifications
- Product specifications
- Educational resources

Trust is earned through consistency and expertise.

---

# Content Freshness

Knowledge should remain current.

Important pages should be reviewed regularly.

Updates should improve accuracy rather than simply change publication dates.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence supports SEO by assisting with:

- Search intent analysis
- Topic research
- Metadata generation
- Internal linking
- Content optimisation
- Structured content validation

Artificial Intelligence supports execution.

Business expertise creates authority.

---

# Future Development

Future versions may include:

- AI Search Optimisation
- Entity-Based SEO
- Semantic Search
- Knowledge Graph Integration
- Voice Search
- Multi-language SEO
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)

without changing the underlying SEO philosophy.

---

# Single Source of Truth

SEO Structure defines how business knowledge becomes discoverable through search.

Knowledge creates authority.

Authority creates trust.

Trust creates sustainable visibility.

Search success is the result of structured knowledge rather than optimisation techniques.
