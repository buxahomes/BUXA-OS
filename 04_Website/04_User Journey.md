# Purpose

This document defines the customer journey across the BUXA digital ecosystem.

The objective is not simply to increase website conversions.

The objective is to guide customers from initial awareness to long-term trust through a structured and consistent experience.

Every interaction should reduce uncertainty and increase confidence.

---

# Vision

A customer journey is not a sales funnel.

It is a confidence-building process.

Every stage should help customers better understand:

- The product
- The project
- The process
- The people
- The value of choosing BUXA

The website is one part of the overall customer journey.

---

# Journey Philosophy

Customers rarely make important purchasing decisions immediately.

They learn.

They compare.

They ask questions.

They build confidence.

The role of BUXA is to support this journey through knowledge rather than sales pressure.

Trust grows before commitment.

---

# Journey Principles

## Customer First

Every journey begins with a customer question.

The objective is to provide answers before requesting action.

---

## Reduce Uncertainty

Every interaction should reduce:

- Product uncertainty
- Installation uncertainty
- Project uncertainty
- Supplier uncertainty

Confidence grows as uncertainty decreases.

---

## Progressive Trust

Trust is earned gradually.

Every interaction should strengthen the customer's confidence.

---

## Consistent Experience

Customers should experience the same brand values across:

- Website
- Social Media
- Email
- Sales
- Sample Box
- Project Delivery
- Aftercare

The experience should feel connected.

---

# Customer Journey

The BUXA customer journey consists of nine stages.

Discover

↓

Learn

↓

Trust

↓

Experience

↓

Decide

↓

Receive

↓

Deliver

↓

Maintain

↓

Recommend

Each stage has a different objective.

---

# Stage 01

## Discover

Customers become aware of BUXA through:

- Google Search
- Google Ads
- Social Media
- Pinterest
- YouTube
- Referrals
- Designers

Objective

Generate curiosity.

Primary Question

Who is BUXA?

---

# Stage 02

## Learn

Customers begin researching.

Typical content includes:

- Collections
- Flooring Guides
- Style Guides
- FAQs
- Quality Assurance
- Product Pages

Objective

Build understanding.

Primary Question

Is this the right product?

---

# Stage 03

## Trust

Customers evaluate credibility.

Trust signals include:

- Manufacturing Standards
- Certifications
- Quality Assurance
- Project Process
- Transparent Information
- Educational Content

Objective

Build confidence.

Primary Question

Can I trust BUXA?

---

# Stage 04

## Experience

Customers experience the products before purchase.

Examples include:

- Sample Box
- Colour Comparison
- Product Consultation
- Designer Discussion

Objective

Reduce purchasing uncertainty.

Primary Question

Will this work in my home?

---

# Stage 05

## Decide

Customers prepare to make a decision.

Support includes:

- Product Comparison
- Technical Advice
- Quotation
- Project Consultation

Objective

Support confident decisions.

Primary Question

Which option is best?

---

# Stage 06

## Receive

Customers receive products and project preparation support.

Examples include:

- Order Confirmation
- Delivery Updates
- Installation Preparation
- Project Planning

Objective

Prepare for successful delivery.

Primary Question

What happens next?

---

# Stage 07

## Deliver

Project delivery begins.

The customer experiences:

- Professional Installation
- Flooring Project Engineer
- Inspection Process
- Quality Assurance
- Project Evidence

Objective

Deliver confidence through professional execution.

Primary Question

Is everything being done correctly?

---

# Stage 08

## Maintain

Following project completion, customers receive:

- Maintenance Guidance
- Warranty Support
- Technical Assistance
- Aftercare Program

Objective

Protect long-term value.

Primary Question

How do I care for my flooring?

---

# Stage 09

## Recommend

Satisfied customers become advocates.

Examples include:

- Referrals
- Designer Recommendations
- Repeat Projects
- Testimonials
- Future Renovations

Objective

Build long-term relationships.

Primary Question

Would I recommend BUXA?

---

# Journey Metrics

The objective is not simply to measure conversions.

Success should also be measured by:

- Customer Confidence
- Knowledge Engagement
- Sample Requests
- Consultation Requests
- Customer Satisfaction
- Referral Rate
- Repeat Business

Long-term trust is a more valuable metric than short-term conversions.

- Analysing customer behaviour

Artificial Intelligence supports the journey.

Business philosophy defines the experience.
