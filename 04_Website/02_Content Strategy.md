# Purpose

This document defines the content strategy of the BUXA digital ecosystem.

Content is not created simply to fill webpages.

Its purpose is to educate customers, communicate expertise, build trust and transform business knowledge into valuable digital assets.

Every piece of content should support both business objectives and customer decision-making.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to become the most trusted knowledge source in the engineered timber flooring industry.

The website should not function as an online catalogue.

It should function as an educational platform.

Every article, guide, product page and resource contributes to one connected knowledge ecosystem.

---

# Content Philosophy

Content exists to solve customer problems.

Not to promote products.

Products demonstrate capability.

Knowledge builds trust.

Trust creates long-term business growth.

---

# Content Objectives

Every piece of content should achieve one or more of the following objectives.

## Educate

Help customers understand products, materials and project decisions.

---

## Build Trust

Demonstrate expertise through structured knowledge rather than marketing claims.

---

## Support Decisions

Provide customers with the information required to make confident purchasing decisions.

---

## Improve Search Visibility

Create high-quality content that answers real customer questions and supports long-term organic search performance.

---

## Strengthen the Knowledge Base

Every published page becomes part of the BUXA Business Operating System.

Content is a business asset rather than a marketing asset.

---

# Content Categories

Website content is organised into six primary categories.

## Product Content

Purpose:

Present products clearly and consistently.

Examples:

- Product Pages
- Collection Pages
- Product Specifications
- Installation Compatibility

---

## Educational Content

Purpose:

Teach customers.

Examples:

- Flooring Guides
- Style Guides
- Project Resources
- FAQs

---

## Inspirational Content

Purpose:

Help customers visualise design possibilities.

Examples:

- Project Inspiration
- Interior Styles
- Colour Inspiration
- Design Ideas

---

## Trust Content

Purpose:

Build confidence.

Examples:

- Quality Assurance
- Certifications
- Manufacturing Standards
- Warranty
- Customer Process

---

## Support Content

Purpose:

Support customers before and after purchase.

Examples:

- Installation Guide
- Care & Maintenance
- FAQs
- Sample Box

---

## Business Content

Purpose:

Explain the company and its values.

Examples:

- About BUXA
- Contact
- Brand Story
- PDMS Introduction

# Content Hierarchy

Every content page should follow a logical structure.

Customer Question

↓

Educational Answer

↓

Supporting Evidence

↓

Related Knowledge

↓

Call to Action

The objective is to answer questions before requesting action.

---

# Writing Principles

Content should always be:

Professional

Clear

Educational

Honest

Practical

Concise

Marketing language should never replace technical accuracy.

---

# Content Standards

Every page should have:

- One primary topic
- One target audience
- One search intent
- One primary call to action

Avoid combining unrelated topics into one page.

---

# Knowledge Relationships

Every page should connect to related knowledge.

Typical relationships include:

- Related Products
- Related Guides
- Related FAQs
- Related Collections
- Related Inspiration
- Related Resources

Knowledge should remain interconnected.

---

# Content Lifecycle

Every content asset follows the same lifecycle.

Research

↓

Planning

↓

Creation

↓

Review

↓

Publication

↓

Performance Analysis

↓

Continuous Improvement

Content should evolve with customer needs and business knowledge.

---

# Content Governance

Every content page should include:

- Content Owner
- Last Updated
- Version History
- Review Schedule

Business knowledge should always remain current.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence supports content production by assisting with:

- Content research
- Content drafting
- SEO optimisation
- Metadata generation
- Internal linking
- Content updates

Artificial Intelligence supports execution.

Business knowledge defines the message.
