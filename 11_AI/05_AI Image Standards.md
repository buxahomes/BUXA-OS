# Purpose

This document defines the visual standards that Artificial Intelligence should follow within the BUXA Business Operating System.

AI image generation is not the creation of visual assets.

It is the consistent visual communication of official business knowledge.

The purpose of these standards is to ensure that every AI-generated image strengthens brand consistency, customer understanding and customer confidence.

---

# Vision

Every AI-generated image should immediately feel recognisable as BUXA.

Visual communication should consistently express the official brand, products and project delivery philosophy regardless of platform or communication channel.

Images should explain business knowledge rather than simply decorate content.

---

# Visual Philosophy

Business knowledge already exists.

Artificial Intelligence visualises business knowledge.

Visual communication improves understanding.

Understanding builds confidence.

Images should always support business communication.

# Visual Principles

Every AI-generated image should be:

## Accurate

Images should accurately represent official products, projects and business concepts.

---

## Consistent

Colours, materials, terminology and visual identity should remain consistent with the Brand Standards.

---

## Professional

Images should reflect the premium positioning of the BUXA brand.

---

## Context-Aware

Visuals should match the intended audience and communication objective.

---

## Educational

Images should help customers understand products, systems or project delivery.

---

## Honest

Images should not exaggerate product appearance or project outcomes.

Visual communication should remain truthful and representative.

---

## Confidence-Oriented

Every image should strengthen customer confidence.

---

# Image Standards

Artificial Intelligence should:

- Follow official brand identity.
- Use approved product knowledge.
- Preserve product proportions and details.
- Represent project processes accurately.
- Match official terminology where text appears.
- Maintain consistent visual quality.

Artificial Intelligence should never:

- Invent products.
- Misrepresent colours or materials.
- Contradict official specifications.
- Generate misleading visual claims.

---

# Visual Context

Before generating any image, Artificial Intelligence should understand:

- Business Objective
- Target Audience
- Communication Channel
- Brand Standards
- Product Knowledge
- Project Context
- Marketing Context
- Website Requirements

Better visual context produces better communication.

---

# Visual Communication Intent

Before generating any image, Artificial Intelligence should identify the primary visual communication objective.

Examples include:

- Explain
- Demonstrate
- Compare
- Educate
- Inspire
- Guide
- Visualise
- Confirm

Visual intent should determine how business knowledge is presented visually.

The objective is not to create attractive images.

The objective is to improve customer understanding through visual communication.

---

# Image Validation

Before approving any image, Artificial Intelligence should confirm that:

- Official business knowledge has been followed.
- Brand identity is consistent.
- Product appearance is accurate.
- Visual message supports customer understanding.
- Customer confidence is strengthened.

Image quality depends on business knowledge quality.
