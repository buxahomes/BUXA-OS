Status: Approved

Owner: Founder

Last Updated: July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence collaborates within the BUXA Business Operating System.

AI collaboration is not the interaction between multiple AI models.

It is the coordinated execution of business capabilities by specialised AI Workforce members.

The purpose of AI Collaboration is to ensure that complex business activities remain consistent, efficient and aligned with official business knowledge.

---

# Vision

Artificial Intelligence should operate as one coordinated digital workforce.

Each AI Agent should remain specialised.

Business capabilities should be combined dynamically according to the business objective.

Collaboration should improve consistency rather than complexity.

---

# Collaboration Philosophy

Business knowledge remains modular.

Business capabilities remain specialised.

Artificial Intelligence coordinates execution.

Quality Assurance validates execution.

Business learning improves future collaboration.

The objective is one business outcome rather than multiple AI outputs.

---

# Collaboration Principles

Every collaborative task should:

- Begin with one business objective.
- Select only the required business capabilities.
- Retrieve official business knowledge.
- Maintain terminology consistency.
- Preserve concept ownership.
- Follow AI Governance.
- Pass Business QA before completion.

Collaboration should strengthen business consistency.

---

# Capability Planning

Before execution, Artificial Intelligence should identify which business capabilities are required.

Examples include:

| Business Objective | Required AI Workforce |
|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Product Page | Website + Product + SEO |
| Collection Page | Website + Product + Marketing + SEO |
| Buying Guide | Product + Marketing + Website + SEO |
| Marketing Campaign | Marketing + Brand + SEO |
| Google Ads | Marketing + SEO |
| Customer Consultation | Sales + Product + Brand |
| Designer Proposal | Sales + Product + PDMS |
| Project Inspection | PDMS + Product |
| Knowledge Review | Knowledge Manager + QA |

Only the required capabilities should participate.

---

# AI Workforce Composition

An AI Workforce should be assembled dynamically according to the business objective.

Simple business tasks may require one AI Agent.

Standard business tasks may require two to four specialised AI Agents.

Complex business activities may require coordinated execution across multiple business capabilities.

The objective is to create the smallest effective AI Workforce for each business objective.

Efficient collaboration improves quality, speed and maintainability.

---

# Knowledge Assembly

Each participating AI Agent retrieves only the knowledge required for its responsibility.

Knowledge may include:

- Brand Standards
- Product Knowledge
- Website Standards
- Marketing Strategy
- Search Strategy
- PDMS Standards
- Customer Context
- Project Context
- Previous Business Learning

Knowledge should remain modular.

No AI Agent should load unnecessary knowledge.

---

# Collaboration Responsibilities

Every AI Agent should:

- Perform one business capability.
- Respect the responsibilities of other AI Agents.
- Share only official business knowledge.
- Preserve terminology consistency.
- Contribute to one business outcome.

Collaboration is based on capability ownership rather than role overlap.

---

# Collaboration Validation

Before completing any collaborative task, Artificial Intelligence should confirm that:

- Every required capability participated.
- Business knowledge is complete.
- Terminology remains consistent.
- Business standards have been followed.
- Quality Assurance has been completed.
- Human review requirements have been respected.

Collaboration quality depends upon capability coordination.
