# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence creates, transforms and manages visual media within the BUXA Operating System.

Media assets communicate approved Business Knowledge through visual expression.

Artificial Intelligence should generate media that accurately represents Business Knowledge while maintaining brand consistency, quality and traceability.

Media supports Business Knowledge.

Media never replaces Business Knowledge.

---

# Vision

BUXA aims to build an AI-native media production system.

Artificial Intelligence should generate professional visual assets that:

- communicate knowledge
- strengthen brand consistency
- increase customer understanding
- maximise asset reuse
- support future automation

Every media asset should become a reusable organisational asset.

---

# Media Philosophy

Business Knowledge remains authoritative.

Media visualises Business Knowledge.

Artificial Intelligence generates visual communication.

People approve visual communication.

Visual presentation may evolve.

Business Knowledge must remain unchanged.

- Brand Positioning
- Product Representation
