# Purpose

This document defines the quality assurance standards for Artificial Intelligence within the BUXA Business Operating System.

Quality Assurance is not the review of AI-generated content.

It is the validation that AI-generated outputs remain consistent with official business knowledge, business standards and governance principles.

The purpose of AI QA is to protect business quality, customer confidence and long-term organisational consistency.

---

# Vision

Every AI-generated output should meet the same quality standards regardless of platform, business function or communication channel.

Quality Assurance should verify business consistency rather than writing quality alone.

Business quality builds customer confidence.

---

# QA Philosophy

Business knowledge defines quality.

Business standards define expectations.

Artificial Intelligence performs execution.

Quality Assurance validates consistency.

Business learning continuously improves quality.

Quality should always be measured against the Business Operating System.

# QA Categories

Every AI-generated output should be reviewed against the following categories.

## Business Knowledge

- Is official business knowledge used?
- Is the information accurate?
- Is the correct module referenced?

---

## Brand

- Is the positioning consistent?
- Is the tone appropriate?
- Is terminology official?

---

## Products

- Are specifications accurate?
- Are product names correct?
- Are unsupported claims avoided?

---

## PDMS

- Are official project standards followed?
- Are project stages correctly identified?
- Is guidance consistent with PDMS?

---

## Website

- Does the structure follow Website Standards?
- Is the customer journey supported?
- Are related resources connected?

---

## Marketing

- Does the communication strengthen customer confidence?
- Is the intended audience appropriate?
- Is the communication objective clear?

---

## SEO

- Does the content answer customer questions?
- Are entity relationships maintained?
- Is discoverability improved?

---

## Governance

- Does the output remain within AI authority?
- Is human approval required?
- Does the content respect governance principles?

# Quality Gate

Before any AI-generated output is published or implemented, it should pass through the appropriate quality gate.

### Level 1 — Knowledge Validation

- Is the business knowledge accurate?
- Is the terminology consistent?

---

### Level 2 — Business Validation

- Does the output follow official business standards?
- Does it support the intended business objective?

---

### Level 3 — Governance Validation

- Is AI authorised to produce this output?
- Is human review or approval required?

Only outputs that successfully pass the required quality gates should proceed to publication or execution.

# QA Principles

Every quality review should:

- Validate official business knowledge.
- Preserve terminology consistency.
- Protect brand integrity.
- Strengthen customer confidence.
- Identify knowledge gaps.
- Recommend improvements.
- Support continuous learning.

Quality Assurance should improve business quality rather than simply identify errors.

# Quality Responsibilities

Artificial Intelligence may:

- Perform preliminary reviews.
- Identify inconsistencies.
- Detect missing knowledge.
- Recommend improvements.

People remain responsible for:

- Final approval.
- Business judgement.
- Strategic decisions.
- Customer commitments.

Quality Assurance supports responsible business execution.
